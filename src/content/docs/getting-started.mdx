Welcome to ZDR Chat! Getting started takes less than a minute.

## Step 1: Get an OpenRouter API Key

1. Go to [OpenRouter.ai](https://openrouter.ai/keys)
2. Create an account (if you don't have one)
3. Generate an API key
4. Copy the key to your clipboard

> **Tip:** If you want zero data retention, visit [OpenRouter Privacy Settings](https://openrouter.ai/settings/privacy) and enable ZDR at the account level + guardrail.

## Step 2: Open ZDR Chat

Go to [app.zdr.chat](https://app.zdr.chat). There's no sign-up, no account creation — just an input field waiting for your API key.

## Step 3: Paste Your Key

Paste your OpenRouter API key into the input field. The key stays in your browser — it's never sent anywhere except directly to OpenRouter when you make a request.

## Step 4: Pick Your First Model

After entering your key, you'll see the model picker. Browse the categories:

- **Smartest** — Top-tier reasoning models
- **Fastest** — Quick response models for everyday use
- **Free** — Models available at no cost
- **Code** — Specialized coding models
- **Vision** — Models that can analyze images

Click any model to select it. You can switch models at any time, even mid-conversation.

## Step 5: Start Chatting

Type your message and press Enter. Your conversation will be saved in your browser's IndexedDB. You can:

- Create multiple conversations (shown in the sidebar)
- Search through your conversation history
- Switch between conversations
- Export or delete conversations
